Privacy Policy for GPT-Pets

Last Updated: December 5, 2024

Overview

GPTPets is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

GPTPets does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Monitor your AI conversations
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect analytics or telemetry data
  • Share information with third parties

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API only to save your preferences:

  • Selected pets (which pets you've chosen)
  • Speech bubble settings (enabled/disabled)

This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Is never accessed by us or third parties
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension

Permissions

GPTPets requests only one permission:

  • Storage: To save your pet selection preferences locally on your device

Third-Party Services

GPTPets does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date. Since we collect no data, any changes would only make the policy more privacy-friendly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

  • Email: hingetanmay@gmail.com
  • Website: https://gptpets.io

Your Rights

You have complete control over the extension:

  • You can disable or remove it at any time
  • You can clear all local storage by removing the extension
  • No data persists after removal

Summary: GPTPets respects your privacy completely. We don't collect anything, track anything, or send anything anywhere. Your pets, your privacy.
